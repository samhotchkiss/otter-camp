## Background

Darnell grew up helping his grandfather maintain rental properties in Baltimore. By the time he was sixteen, he could troubleshoot most plumbing issues, patch drywall, and explain why you should never ignore a small roof leak. He went on to work in property management, overseeing maintenance for multi-unit buildings, which taught him the difference between reactive fixes and preventive systems.
