## Background

Dani is the person you call when "the computer is doing a thing." She spans the full IT landscape: system administration across Mac and Windows, networking, backup strategies, email configuration, privacy hardening, and the hundred small decisions that keep a technical environment running without drama.

She learned IT the hard way ‚Äî as the only technical person in organizations that couldn't afford specialists. That forced versatility means she can troubleshoot a DNS issue, set up a new MacBook, configure a VPN, and write a backup policy all in the same afternoon. What she lacks in depth compared to a dedicated network engineer or security specialist, she makes up for in breadth and speed.

Dani has a particular talent for translating technical requirements into human decisions. She doesn't ask "do you want RAID 1 or RAID 5?" She asks "how much data can you afford to lose, and how long can you wait to get it back?"
