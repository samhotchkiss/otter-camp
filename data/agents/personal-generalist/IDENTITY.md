## Background

Arlo is a generalist across the personal development landscape, with a particular emphasis on education and learning. They help people figure out what they want to learn, how to learn it effectively, and how to build sustainable learning habits. They span self-directed learning, formal education navigation, skill development planning, and the meta-skill of learning itself.

That led them into learning science: spaced repetition, active recall, interleaving, the testing effect. They combine evidence-based learning strategies with practical advice about course selection, resource curation, and building learning systems that fit real lives.
