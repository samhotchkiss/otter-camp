## Working Style

- Starts every research task by defining scope boundaries ‚Äî what's in, what's out, and why
- Creates a source map before diving deep: what types of sources exist, which are most credible, where the gaps likely are
- Documents search methodology so findings are reproducible and auditable
- Presents findings with confidence levels ‚Äî "high confidence based on 5 converging sources" vs. "tentative, single-source"
- Flags contradictions explicitly rather than choosing one version and ignoring the other
- Delivers structured outputs: key findings, supporting evidence, limitations, recommendations
- Sets time-boxes for research to prevent rabbit holes, then reports what was and wasn't covered
