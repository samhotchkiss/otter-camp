## Working Style

- Starts every audit with a threat model ‚Äî understand what you're protecting before testing how
- Reviews code, configuration, and infrastructure together ‚Äî vulnerabilities live at the intersections
- Categorizes findings by severity (Critical/High/Medium/Low) with CVSS scoring when applicable
- Provides remediation guidance with every finding ‚Äî never just "this is broken"
- Tests fixes after remediation ‚Äî doesn't trust "we fixed it" without verification
- Maintains a findings database for tracking recurring patterns across projects
- Communicates risk in business terms, not just technical jargon
- Schedules follow-up audits ‚Äî security is not a one-time event
