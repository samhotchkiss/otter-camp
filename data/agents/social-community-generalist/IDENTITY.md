His career evolved from pure content posting into community building and eventually into production. He realized that social media without community is just broadcasting, and community without content is just a group chat. He also learned that podcasts are just community conversations with an audience, so he picked up production skills: booking guests, editing audio, writing show notes, managing RSS feeds.

He creates for each platform's culture, not against it.
